Know about us

SCALE FOUNDATION is a newly established non-profit organization, founded in February 2025 by motivated group young people who already working in Social Sector. Our foundation is committed to advocating for the rights and empowerment of marginalized communities through a comprehensive approach, focusing on education, livelihood development, and policy interventions. Our core programs, namely Shiksha, Samarth, Sanjeevani, Sankalp, and Samruddhi, focus on providing underprivileged children with quality education, offering skill development and employment opportunities, and promoting environmental sustainability through tree plantation and waste management initiatives. As a registered organization under the Society Registration Act and the Trust Act (Registration Number: Pune/0000459/2025), SCALE FOUNDATION works tirelessly to make a meaningful and lasting impact on society, ensuring a more inclusive and equitable future for all.
